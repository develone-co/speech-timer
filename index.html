Timer Role Introduction

Read this aloud at the start of the meeting

Hello, fellow Toastmasters and welcome guests. As your Timer today, my role is to help our speakers stay within their allotted time.

I'll be using a colour-coded timing system that works like a traffic light:

GREEN means you've reached your minimum time — you're within the window, well done!

ORANGE means you've hit the closing midpoint — it's time to start wrapping up your thoughts.

RED means you've reached your maximum time — please conclude your speech.

If you continue past the red, the screen will flash as a gentle reminder to wrap up.

For today's prepared speeches, the timing is 5 to 7 minutes. For Table Topics, it's 1 to 2 minutes. And for evaluations, it's 2 to 3 minutes.

I'll display the timer where everyone can see it. Speakers — please keep an eye on the colours so you can deliver your best speech within the time. Good luck to everyone!
